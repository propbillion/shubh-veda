Questions

Answered.

When is SHUBH VEDA launching? +

SHUBH VEDA launches in 2026 with pre-launch bookings open now to a select set of pre-registered patrons. Early registrants receive priority allocation and best-in-launch pricing.

Where is the project located? +

SHUBH VEDA is located in Akurdi, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Pune. Minutes from the Akurdi Metro Station and Akurdi Railway Station, with seamless connectivity to Hinjewadi, the airport, and central Pune.

What residence types are available? +

3 BHK, 3.5 BHK, 4 BHK, 4.5 BHK, and signature 5 BHK sky residences. Carpet areas range from 1106 sqft to 3200 sqft across seven towers.

How many amenities does the project offer? +

70+ lifestyle amenities spread across four clubhouses, all positioned above the metro line on Pune's largest amenity podium. 70 percent of the 13-acre site is open space.

What is the possession timeline? +

Possession timelines vary tower-wise and are disclosed per MahaRERA registration. Detailed handover schedules are shared with serious buyers post pre-registration.

How do I book a site visit? +

Site visits are by appointment. Message us on WhatsApp at +91 88570 90799 and our concierge will arrange a private visit at your preferred time.

Is the project a good investment? +

Akurdi has shown consistent appreciation, accelerated by metro connectivity and infrastructure growth. Pre-launch pricing offers the strongest entry point in the project's lifecycle.
